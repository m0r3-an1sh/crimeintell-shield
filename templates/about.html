Our Story

I am a crime analytics startup, born out of a passion for using technology to fight crime.We began by analyzing public crime data and identifying patterns in criminal activity.

  • We created AI to predict where crimes are likely to occur.
  • Alert the authorities if the crime is detected.
  • Alert trustee's of a user in case of emergencies.

Crime intelligence, also known as criminal intelligence, is the process of gathering, analyzing, and disseminating information about criminal activity to help law enforcement agencies prevent, investigate, and solve crimes.

Criminal identification

Crime identification refers to the process of determining characteristics of a crime, as well as identifying the person or group responsible for committing it.

Scope and Nature of Crimes

The scope and nature of criminal activity refers to the types and frequency of crimes committed, as well as the characteristics of those who commit them

Potential Criminal Threats

Criminal threat identification involves using various methods and tools to identify individuals or groups who may pose a threat to public safety.

Crime Trends Analysis

Crime trends refer to patterns or changes in the frequency and type of criminal activity over a specific period of time.
